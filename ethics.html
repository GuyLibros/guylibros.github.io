⚖️ Ethics and Academic Integrity with AI

As helpful as generative AI can be, it’s important to use these tools ethically and responsibly, especially in academic settings. Here’s how to stay on the right path.

Best Practices

  • Be Transparent: Acknowledge when you use AI assistance in your work.
  • Cite Appropriately: If AI-generated text significantly contributes to your writing, cite the tool (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini).
  • Verify Information: AI can make mistakes or invent facts. Always fact-check important claims or statistics.
  • Don’t Use AI to Plagiarize: Submitting AI-generated work as your own may violate academic integrity policies.
